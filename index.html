About me

Meet Teacher Nini

Hi! My name is Nina and I am 22 years old. I am a certified teacher and I have been working for over 3 years. I am TESOL and TEFL certified and I taught students of all ages. I am also certified for teaching IELTS lessons. I help my students improve their speaking, reading and writing skills. I love teaching and I enjoy doing my job. I strive to help my students accomplish their goals and I am always there for them! I believe in creating and providing a pleasant and inspiring atmosphere. I am patient and intuitive with a relaxed and encouraging teaching style. I use many props, such as cards and toys. Thank you very much for choosing me as your teacher. I look forward to making our lessons fun, engaging and fulfilling. I am excited to meet you and see you shine in and out of my English classroom.
